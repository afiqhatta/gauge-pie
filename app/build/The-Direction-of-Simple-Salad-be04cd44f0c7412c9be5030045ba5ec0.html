Lots of news websites only criticize, but don’t offer solutions. This is a bit confusing to me. News websites are privy to lots of information that could help with the problems that they report. However, they don’t offer recommendations. This is suboptimal - they have large audiences to act on the recommendations that they publish


Most people read news, but then don’t act on possibly privileged information that they have. However, some newspapers like the New York Times have indeed done this.

Should newspapers be more active in designing technology and solutions for problems?

In addition, should there be more active work in trying to increase the level of interactivity in news? For example, distill.pub has a great article on interactivity in scientific articles. https://distill.pub/2020/communicating-with-interactive-articles/
