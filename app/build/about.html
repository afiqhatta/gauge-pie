About

I'm Afiq Hatta. Currently, I am a Part III Mathematics student at Trinity Hall, University of Cambridge. Right now, I'm studying the problems that crop up in quantum field theory, string theory, and theoretical condensed matter, but I aspire to work in trading! I made this website to document some mathematics and physics that I've found interesting.

I find explaining things a great way to learn.

As an undergraduate at Cambridge, I've had the opportunity to intern in

  • London at Goldman Sachs
  • Oxford at Oxford Asset Management
  • Kuala Lumpur for the Malaysian Central Bank
These internships have been thoroughly mathematical, and have been within in a quantitative research capacity. Roughly, they have involved on scientific computing, optimisation, and natural language processing.
